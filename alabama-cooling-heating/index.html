FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers to the questions we hear most often.

How often should I service my HVAC system?

We recommend two tune-ups per year — once in spring for your AC and once in fall for your heating system. Regular maintenance catches small issues before they become expensive breakdowns and keeps your equipment running efficiently. Ask us about our maintenance plans that bundle both visits at a discount.

What brands of HVAC equipment do you service?

We service all major brands including Trane, Carrier, Lennox, Goodman, Rheem, York, American Standard, Bryant, and many more. Our technicians are trained on both modern and older systems, so don't worry if your unit has a few years on it — we can still get parts and keep it running.

Do you offer financing on new installations?

Yes — we offer flexible financing options through trusted lending partners so a new system doesn't have to mean a big upfront expense. We'll walk you through the options during your free in-home estimate. Ask about seasonal promotions too.

How fast can you get to an emergency call?

Most emergency calls in Oneonta and nearby towns are handled same-day, often within a couple of hours. For truly critical situations — no AC in 95° heat or no heat during a freeze — we dispatch 24/7 including weekends and holidays. Call (205) 395-8584 and we'll give you a clear ETA.

Do you offer maintenance plans?

We do. Our maintenance plans include twice-yearly tune-ups, priority scheduling, and discounts on repairs and new installations. It's the easiest way to protect your investment and avoid surprise failures during extreme weather. Call or ask for details at your next service visit.
