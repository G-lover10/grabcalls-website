About Beyond Blessed

Welcome to Beyond Blessed Child Care, where Director Cynthia "Cindi" Ahl has created a warm, nurturing home away from home for children ages 0-14. With over 15 dedicated staff members and our Alabama Quality STARS rating, we provide faith-based care grounded in Christian values.

Our center is built on love, trust, and a commitment to helping every child reach their full potential. We believe each child is a unique blessing, and we create an environment where children thrive academically, socially, and spiritually.

Frequently Asked Questions

Beyond Blessed Child Care is open Monday through Friday, 6:00 AM to 5:30 PM. This extended schedule accommodates working parents and provides before/after school care for older children. Holidays vary — please ask during your tour.

Tuition includes care, educational programming, snacks, and age-appropriate materials. Meals beyond snacks, additional supplies, and special programs (field trips, tutoring) may have additional costs. Contact us for a detailed rate sheet.

We offer full-time (5 days/week), part-time, and drop-in care options. Our goal is to work with families on their unique needs. Please call Director Cindi at (205) 849-8696 to discuss your situation.

We follow Alabama Department of Public Health guidelines. Children with fever, vomiting, or contagious rashes should stay home until symptom-free. We communicate immediately if a child becomes ill during care. Detailed policies provided at enrollment.

Yes, Beyond Blessed Child Care is fully licensed by the State of Alabama, maintains comprehensive liability insurance, and holds an Alabama Quality STARS rating. Safety, compliance, and excellence are our highest priorities.

We provide daily updates via text/email, including photos, meals eaten, and activities. Monthly parent-teacher conferences are scheduled. Director Cindi is always available for questions and concerns at (205) 849-8696.
